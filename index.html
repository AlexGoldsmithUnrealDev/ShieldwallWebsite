What is Shieldwall Games Ltd?

Shieldwall Games Ltd is an indie game development studio currently developing Ashes of Yggdrasil. So what is Ashes of Yggdrasil

  • Viking Village management Sim
  • Survival elements and realistic reprecussions
  • Deep role playing system
  • Hunt, skin, tame and breed mosterous beasts
  • Work with NPC or friends in Multiplayer Co-Op
  • Build a village, train your settlers, launch raids, become rich and become the Legend
